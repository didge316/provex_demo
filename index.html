How ProveX Works

cableConnect Your Payment Account You can reserve your tokens before you need them by sending a payment through the app you already use and trust. Whether it’s Venmo, Zelle, Cash App, or another platform, just choose the one that’s most convenient for you. This way, your tokens will be ready and waiting when you want to use them — no delays, no extra steps.
new_labelGenerate Zero-Knowledge Proof You can prove that a payment really happened without revealing private details. This uses a cryptographic method called a zero-knowledge proof (zk proof). It confirms the transaction is valid while hiding sensitive data like who sent it, the amount, or account numbers — letting you prove payment securely without exposing personal information.
paidInstant Trustless Settlement Once the payment proof is confirmed on the blockchain, the escrowed cryptocurrency is **automatically released in full** — no manual approval, no delays. The verified proof triggers an instant, all-or-nothing transfer to the correct recipient.
